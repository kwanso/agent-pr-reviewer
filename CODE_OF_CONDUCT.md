# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community and associated projects will experience harassment-free participation based on factors including, but not limited to, their identity and expression, background, experience, gender identity and expression, marital status, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, socioeconomic status, veteran status, or technical choices.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting others' boundaries and privacy
- Acknowledging and correcting mistakes when pointed out

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Unwelcome physical contact
- Any kind of discrimination or bias

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include using an official email address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to:

**Email:** conduct@kwanso.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of the reporter of any incident. Further details of specific enforcement policies may be posted separately.

**Response Time:** We will respond to all reports within 48 hours.

## Consequences

Those who do not follow the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by community leadership.
